KAIROS
/ˈkaɪrɒs/ · the opportune moment

A consortium to predict platinum response in high-grade serous ovarian cancer.

One in five HGSOC patients won't respond to platinum. New therapies are here, from PARP inhibitors to ADCs to immunotherapy. Matching patients to them needs a biomarker we can trust.

KAIROS is that biomarker. A multimodal AI foundation model being built by leading cancer centers. Our goal: pool 10,000 HGSOC patients to train it, together.

Digital personalised medicine, at scale.
